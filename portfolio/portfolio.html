About Me:

Greetings! I am Natalie Farrell - a software developer residing in Portland, OR.

Experienced Freelance Artist with a demonstrated history of working in the graphic design industry. Skilled in Visual Arts, Adobe Photoshop, Dreamweaver, and Social Media. Strong arts and design professional graduated from MiraCosta College.

Growing up in San Diego, CA, I went to school studying Multimedia Arts and Technologies. Here in Portland, I'm studying at The Tech Academy.

I enjoy digging my hands into different aspects of coding and creativity, as well as gaining as much knowledge as I can. I'm experienced with: HTML5 & CSS, Version Control, JavaScript, SQL, Python, C#, and .Net Framework..

I am passionate about computers, art, animals, music, helping others, and keeping a fit/healthy lifestyle. On my spare time I enjoy playing video games, spontaneous adventures, and being with my family, my dog, my two snakes, and friends.
